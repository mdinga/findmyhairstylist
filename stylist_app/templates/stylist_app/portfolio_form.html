How to add picture to your Gallery

Scroll down to add a picture.

  1. Select 'Choose file' and select a picture from your phone or PC
  2. Under 'Hairstyle Label' select name of the hairstyle in the picture
  3. Under 'Description' add additional information about the hairstyle. This is optional.
  4. Press 'Submit'. Your picture will appear on your gallery
